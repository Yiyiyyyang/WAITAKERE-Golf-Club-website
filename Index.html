Waitekere Golf Club

Welcome To The Waitakere Golf Club

A million miles from the city but only 30 mins by car the Waitakere Golf Course is a peaceful sanctuary from the daily grind. Nestled in the magnificent Waitakere Ranges surrounded by native bush and birds the course has been developed with minimal change to the natural landscape.

A Hidden Gem

Waitakere Golf Club is hidden in a small corner of the Cascade Kauri Park, 30 minutes drive from Auckland Central City. We have a very special 18 hole golf course with 4 par 5's and a stunning 6th hole where you tee off in the bush. No two holes are similar and the course offers a wonderful experience for golfers of all abilities.
